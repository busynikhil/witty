At Witty we are a Happy School, nurturing and developing children’s inner resilience and life skills alongside their academic progression.

All round development of children with focus on their physical, emotional, intellectual, technological and social development and well-being is central to WIS education. This opens up a vast array of opportunities for students to go beyond the learning of the core academic subjects.

We are dedicated to cultivating and preparing principled, compassionate and confident young people who will become globally responsible citizens. Our balanced world class education, with a unique focus on wellness, fosters respect and harmony for self and others; and, empowers students to imagine, explore and achieve a peaceful, sustainable and happy future.
